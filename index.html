SherriBerryApps

After attending 12-week boot camp this year, I decided that what I'd really like to do is develop apps. I'm working on a couple right now. One is a travel app that makes it easy for people to share their road trip with the folks left behind. Another is an easy and fun budget and money management system for creatives. The learning journey is endless. There are so many exciting and fun software tools I'm learning such as Material Design, MongoDB, Nodejs, Meteor, AngularJS, while I continue to practice the basics, JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

Journalism

I've been a journalist for more than 15 years. Most recently, I was an editor for the Orange County Register's university sections. I was also a technology reporter for the Star Tribune and I wrote for the Orange County Business Journal and the Las Vegas Business Press. I've written for local, regional and national magazines as a freelancer. I've just begun writing and editing for CMSWire.

Pig Popular

I'm revising a children's book that I finished last year. Inspired by my daughter, Rhiannon, the book is about a surfing pig named Pig Popular who is challenged by a new student, Mimsy Sage. She is the surfing champ back home and becomes quite popular starting with the first day of third grade. She's coolness. We dreamt up the story while living in Newport Beach, where cool lives.
